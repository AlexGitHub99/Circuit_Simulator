Circuit Simulator

Left Mouse Button - select items from menu and place
Right Mouse Button - edit resistance and voltage
"R" - rotate component
"-" - zoom out
"+" - zoom in

Notice: Circuit simulator can find
resistance and current for only
ONE BATTERY circuits

Any combination of series and parallel
circuits will work, however wheatstone-
bridge type circuits will not work

The ammeter can be placed in any part
of the circuit and will find current
